# Merchant User Guide: Managing Your Stock

Welcome to your Inventory Dashboard. This guide will help you manage your products and track stock levels using the simple interface shown below.

![Inventory Dashboard Overview](/img/inventory-dashboard-screenshot.png)

*Your dashboard features a clear, top-down view of your current session and inventory items.*

---

## Your Session
At the very top of the screen, you can verify which account you are currently using. 
* **Logged in as:** Displays your registered email address.
* **Logout:** Click this black button to securely end your session and protect your data.

---

## Adding a New Product
To add a new item to your records, use the **Add Item** section in the middle of the screen:

1. **Name:** Type the name of the product (e.g., "Headphones") into the text field.
2. **Quantity:** Use the number box to set your starting stock level.
3. **Submit:** Click the wide grey **Add** button. 

*Your new item will immediately appear in the list below.*

---

## Managing Your Items
All your saved products are listed at the bottom under **Items**. Each entry shows the item name and current quantity (e.g., `qty 3`).

### Updating & Removing Items
To the right of every item, you have two primary actions:
* **Edit:** Click this to modify the name or quantity of an existing item.
* **Delete:** Click the red button to permanently remove an item from your inventory. 

:::warning Proceed with Caution
Clicking **Delete** will remove the item immediately. Ensure you no longer need the record before confirming.
:::

---

## Best Practices
* **Consistent Naming:** Use clear names to keep your list organized and searchable.
* **Regular Updates:** Update your quantities whenever you receive new stock to keep your dashboard accurate for your team.
